About Me

The dichotomy between theory and experiment

In the realm of scientific exploration, my fundamental philosophy centers around the vital connection between theoretical understanding and empirical research. With over a decade of unwavering commitment, my primary aim is to bridge the gap between experimental practice and theoretical foundations, particularly within the intricate domain of neuroscience. This philosophy forms the cornerstone of my research endeavors, and it is the lens through which I approach the multifaceted world of scientific inquiry.
