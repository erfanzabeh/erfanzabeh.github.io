Philosophy of Science

Science is not merely a collection of facts, but a dynamic process of inquiry aimed at understanding the natural world. I believe in:

  • Rigorous methodology - Proper research design and statistical rigor are fundamental to valid scientific conclusions.
  • Transparency - Open communication about methods, limitations, and findings strengthens the scientific enterprise.
  • Interdisciplinary thinking - Complex problems benefit from perspectives across multiple disciplines.
  • Continuous learning - Science evolves as we refine our understanding and methods.
  • Social responsibility - Research should contribute positively to society and humanity.
