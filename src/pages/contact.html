Frequently Asked Questions

Are you available for collaborations?

Yes! I'm interested in collaborative research projects, academic partnerships, and educational initiatives. Please reach out with details about your proposal.

Do you accept speaking engagements?

I do accept speaking invitations at academic conferences, universities, and educational events. Please contact me with event details, date, and topic information.

How can I access your research papers?

Most of my publications are available through academic databases and my publication archive. Visit the portfolio section for links to major publications.

Can I cite your work?

Absolutely! All my published work is available for academic citation. Please refer to each publication for proper citation formats and DOI information.

How do I subscribe to your blog?

Visit the blog page for subscription options. You can also check the archives for past articles and discussions on philosophy of science and research methodology.
